About Me

My name is Cameron Walther and I am a student at the UNC Charlotte Coding Bootcamp. I attended the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire where I earned a Bachelor's of Business Administration with a major in Marketing in the Professional Sales Emphasis. During my time there I was a member of the Sales Team, participating in mock role play sales scenarios in a competitive environment. After college I moved to Dallas, Texas to work for McAfee, but I found no happiness there. Shortly after, I went back into the restaurant industry, a position I had worked in throughout college. I believe the coding bootcamp will help me use my excellent logic and problem solving skills to provide a new direction for my life.

In my free time I love reading, doing outdoor activities, playing video games and watching Star Trek. My favorite books are The Fountainhead by Ayn Rand and Moneyball by Michael Lewis. During my time in Austin I loved going to float the San Marcos river, kayaking on Ladybird Lake and spending time listening to live music on patios. I've played a lot of great video games this year from God of War to Spider-Man and Red Dead Redemption 2, but I also love phone games. I find the mobile games really fascinating because we're learning the skills in this bootcamp to create them ourselves eventually. I know it's a cliche and a stereotype at times in this industry to love Star Trek, but it's in my blood to love Star Trek. Both of my parents grew up as huge Trekkies and although they divorced when I was young they both raised me to be the same. My mom even made a Commander William Riker costume for Halloween when I was 4!

Thanks for coming to my page, I hope you like what you see!
